Meet the Founder

The heart behind every home we touch.

Every project begins with compassion, respect, and a commitment to helping Cape Breton families through life's important transitions.

Kayla

Founder & Lead Consultant

I’m Kayla, founder of East Coast Estates & Staging. I help families navigate downsizing, estate transitions, and home clear-outs with care, clarity, and respect. If you’re here, you may be downsizing your own home, supporting a parent through a major transition, settling an estate, or simply unsure where to begin. You’re not alone. For me, this work is about people first. Every home holds a lifetime of memories, and I approach each space with empathy and understanding.

I’ve always loved antiques and vintage pieces, but what matters most are the stories behind them. Whether it’s a treasured heirloom or everyday belongings, I do my best to ensure items are handled thoughtfully and, when possible, given a meaningful next chapter. When I enter your home, I recognize the trust you’re placing in me. I’m here to support you with patience, clear communication, and respect every step of the way.

My promise is simple: I will treat your home, your belongings, and your family with kindness, patience, and respect. We’ll move at your pace, honour the memories, and make the process feel more manageable. It would truly be an honour to support you through this next chapter.
